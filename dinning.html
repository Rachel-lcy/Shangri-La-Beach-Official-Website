A Culinary Journey

Enjoy a variety of dining experiences at Shangri La Beach Resort—from elegant fine dining to relaxed beachside bites, each meal promises delicious flavors and memorable moments.

The Beach House - Fine Dining with Ocean Views

The Beach House offers a fine dining experience with stunning ocean views. Enjoy fresh local seafood and premium global ingredients, crafted into creative dishes by top chefs. Perfect for special occasions or a luxurious night out.

  1. Cuisine: Fine Dining, Seafood, International
  2. Ambiance: Elegant, Romantic, Oceanfront
  3. Dress Code: Smart Casual
  4. Reservations Recommended

The Sand Bar - Relaxed Beachfront Grill

The Sand Bar offers casual beachside dining with a laid-back vibe. Enjoy grilled favorites, fresh salads, and sandwiches with ocean views and your feet in the sand—perfect for lunch, snacks, or a relaxed dinner under the stars.

  1. Cuisine: Casual, Grill, Seafood, American
  2. Ambiance: Relaxed, Beachfront, Informal
  3. Dress Code: Casual
  4. No Reservations Required

The Poolside Cafe - Refreshments and Light Bites

The Poolside Café keeps you refreshed with light bites like salads, wraps, and snacks, plus tropical cocktails and chilled drinks. Grab a quick meal or sip by the pool without missing a moment in the sun.

  1. Cuisine: Casual, Light Bites, Snacks, Beverages
  2. Ambiance: Relaxed, Poolside, Informal
  3. Dress Code: Casual
  4. No Reservations Required

The Lobby Lounge - Sophisticated Cocktails and Ambiance

The Lobby Lounge is a chic spot to relax with handcrafted cocktails, fine wines, and small bites. Unwind in style, enjoy live music, and soak in the elegant ambiance—perfect for starting or ending your evening.

  1. Cuisine: Beverages, Light Appetizers
  2. Ambiance: Sophisticated, Relaxed, Live Music (select evenings)
  3. Dress Code: Smart Casual
  4. No Reservations Required
